Design Maturity Survey

This survey helps to understand how the design profession is understood and perceived across the Department for Education (DfE).

For the purpose of this survey, design covers Service Design, Content Design and Interaction Design.

This survey is completely anonymous and shouldn't take longer than 5 minutes to complete.

Complete the survey

This survey closes at 11:59pm on 14 March.

Start

Who should complete this survey

  • Anyone who works in a design role
  • Anyone in a DDaT role including product, research, delivery, technical, or architecture
  • Anyone in a data, analysis, or analytical role
  • Anyone outside of digital roles, but might work with designers, for example, policy or strategy

Depending on your role, you may not know the answers for all of the questions When in doubt, pick the option that best matches your perception.

What we do with responses

We'll use the information from all responses to understand where to focus our efforts in improving design understanding in DfE.

We'll publish analysis of the results at the end of March.

If you have any questions about this survey, contact Andy Jones (Head of Design).
